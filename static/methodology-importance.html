Why is auditing journals important?

Of the 36 standards which constitute the framework for a statutory audit, it often seems like none generates as much discussion as ISA 240, dealing with the auditor's responsibilities relating to fraud.

One of the key parts of ISA 240 is the requirement to test a sample of the journals themselves, focusing particularly on journals with particular characteristics which may be indicative of a fraud risk.

However, many auditors find this difficult.
